Barbara

Hello, I am Barbara.
At the time of Santa’s… incident, I was in the kitchen preparing a Christmas cake. I like things neat and orderly, so I tend to get absorbed in what I’m doing. My husband had popped out for an hour — he said he was meeting some friends for a pint, though he didn’t mention which ones.
He came back looking unusually pale, almost shaken. He brushed it off and said the cold had “got to him,” but I’m not sure I believed him. He’s normally very composed… trained to be, really. Sometimes he forgets he’s not supposed to bring his work mindset home.
Earlier he joked to you about becoming a “hit man.” He shouldn’t really make jokes like that, not with the sort of work he does — but he’s always had a strange sense of humour. I know he works for the …well, a government bureau, and they’re very particular about discretion, so I shouldn’t say too much.
Anyway, that’s all I know.
