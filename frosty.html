Frosty the Snowman

This is Frosty the snowman.
He will not talk because after the murder his voice froze.

Their description

He is white with black buttons, a red scarf and have a carrot for a nose.
He also wears a blue hat.

Notes

Apparently he was seen at the crime scene.
Not many people trust him as he is 'cold hearted'and worry he may try to freeze them.
