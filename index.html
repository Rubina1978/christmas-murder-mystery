Welcome to our Christmas Murder Mystery

We need your help!
There has been a murder, someone here is guilty, and unless the truth is uncovered, Christmas is finished.
We need you to 'interview' the suspects by SAY HOW HERE Things to think about and help you in your investigation:

  • What is the suspects name?
  • What was their relationship to the victim?
  • Where were they at the time of the murder?
  • Did they have any possessions on them at the time of the murder?

“To set the scene and stir up some Christmas cheer, here’s a little poem to begin your adventure.”

T’was the run-up to Christmas, when Noel, half-asleep,
Went to feed reindeer kept warm in the keep.
But there in the rafters he froze where he stood—
For Santa lay lifeless, no longer so good.

A crumpled note reading “SATAN” lay near—
A typo? A warning? A message unclear.
Fairy lights tangled like someone had planned,
And too many motives for one pair of hands.

So gather your wits, pour a drink if you must—
For someone here’s guilty, and someone’s unjust.
The truth must be found ere the night’s icy end…
Or Christmas is cancelled—again, my dear friend.
